About Us

Founded in 2023 by Anthonio Obert Lais, FootLockRE began as a project during the HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) course and evolved into an online marketplace in Indonesia specializing in selling authentic footwear, particularly sneakers.


Officially launched in 2023, FootLockRE is based in Jakarta. With a strong emphasis on authenticity, the platform ensures that customers can trust the quality and genuineness of the footwear they purchase. FootLockRE aims to cater to the growing demand for sneakers and provide a seamless online shopping experience for footwear enthusiasts across Indonesia.
